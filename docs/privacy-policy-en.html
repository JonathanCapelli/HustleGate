🔒 Privacy Policy — HustleGate

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Introduction

HustleGate is an Android digital wellbeing application that blocks access to selected apps and requires the user to perform physical exercises to unlock screen time. This policy describes how the application handles your data.

2. Data Collected

HustleGate does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains exclusively on your device.

Data processed locally:

  • Camera — Used only in real time to detect exercise movements via AI (ML Kit Pose Detection). No images or videos are recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Step data (Health Connect) — The app reads your daily step count via the Health Connect API to award time rewards. This data is neither stored nor shared.
  • Installed app list — Used only to display the list of apps that the user can choose to block. This list is neither stored nor transmitted.
  • Accessibility Service — Used to detect the foreground application in order to enforce the blocking configured by the user. No browsing data is recorded.
  • Preferences — Settings (blocked apps, remaining time, exercise goals) are stored locally on the device via SharedPreferences.

3. Advertising

HustleGate displays banner ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Users can remove ads through an in-app purchase.

4. In-App Purchases

The app offers a one-time purchase to remove ads, managed by Google Play Billing. Transactions are processed by Google Play and are not accessible to the developer.

5. Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with third parties, sold, or transmitted, except for advertising data processed by Google AdMob as described in Section 3.

6. Storage & Security

All application data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all associated data.

7. User Rights

In accordance with the GDPR, you may at any time:

  • Revoke permissions (camera, Health Connect) in your Android settings
  • Uninstall the app to delete all data
  • Disable ad personalization in your device's Google settings

8. Children

HustleGate is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated. The last updated date at the top of this page will be changed accordingly.
